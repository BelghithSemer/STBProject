About STB Bank

Société Tunisienne de Banque (STB) is one of the largest banks in Tunisia, established in 1957. It provides a wide range of banking and financial services, including retail banking, corporate banking, investment banking, and asset management. STB has played a significant role in the economic development of Tunisia by supporting various sectors such as agriculture, industry, and services.

STB is committed to delivering high-quality financial products and services to its customers, leveraging technology and innovation to meet the evolving needs of its clientele. With a strong network of branches across Tunisia, STB continues to be a pillar of the Tunisian banking sector, dedicated to promoting economic growth and financial inclusion.
